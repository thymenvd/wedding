FAQ

 Is the date definite?

Yes, we have already set the date.

 How do I get there? 

It is possible to fly to Bogotá or Cartegena, from there you can rent a car and drive to Barichara or use the bus to get the all Colombian experience (we would recommend renting a car).

 Will it be the entire day?

Yes! But don't worry, we will not start too early. Later on, there will be more information on what the day will look like. All that we can ensure is that you can party until the sun rises.

Where do I stay?

We will provide some recommendations, but in general it is really easy to find a Hotel in Barichara. Just check booking.com

Do I need to speak Spanish?

It is highly recommended to take some basic knowledge of the Spanish language with you. Eventhough Colombia is getting more and more touristic, you will not encounter a lot of people outside the cities who speak English.
