Travel tips

Just to give you an idea of what you could do, we have a suggestion of how you can plan your trip. Colombia is so diverse and gorgeous, just look at https://www.tomplanmytrip.com/ for more ideas and suggestions.

1

Arrive in Bogotá

Start in the capital of Colombia and discover the area where Adriana grew up. You can take some days in Bogotá to recover from the trip. Here you can see places like La Candelaria, Guatavita (the legend of El Dorado), or Monseratte. Keep in mind that Bogotá is 2600m closer to the stars.

2

Travel around

From Bogotá you can easily travel to the Amazon, the coffee region, Guatapé or more remote places like Caño Cristales.

3

Drive to Barichara

If you drive from Bogotá, you can make a stop in the colonial village called Villa de Leyva. From here it is 5 hours driving to Barichara. During this drive you pass through the Chicamocha canyon and enjoy the surroundings:)

4

Continue to the Caribbean cost

After the wedding and required days to recover from your hangover, you can drive to the north to end the trip at the Caribbean cost. Here you can visit places like Parque Tayrona, Islas del Rosario, Baru or La Guajira.

5

Depart from Cartegena

A visit to Colomia will not be complete without visiting the touristic city of Cartagena. Here you van end this epic journey and fly back home.
